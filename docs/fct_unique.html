Unique values of a factor

Usage

fct_unique(f)

Arguments

f
A factor

Description

Unique values of a factor

Examples

f <- factor(letters[rpois(100, 10)])

unique(f)     # in order of appearance


 [1] l h f g d e k j i m q r s n o p
Levels: d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s



fct_unique(f) # in order of levels


 [1] d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s
Levels: d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s
